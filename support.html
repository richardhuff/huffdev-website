Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?
I specialize in web applications, desktop software, APIs, and custom tools. My focus is on creating solutions that solve real problems efficiently and elegantly.
How do you handle project support?
All projects include initial support during launch. Extended support and maintenance packages are available for ongoing needs, including bug fixes, updates, and feature enhancements.
Do you provide source code?
Yes, clients receive full source code for custom projects along with documentation and deployment instructions. This ensures you have complete control over your software.
What operating systems does Monomidi support?
Monomidi is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. The application is built with Electron, ensuring consistent functionality across all platforms.
Can I use Monomidi with my DAW?
Yes! Monomidi integrates with all major DAWs including Ableton Live, Logic Pro, FL Studio, and Pro Tools through standard MIDI protocols.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, Monomidi offers a 14-day free trial with full functionality. No credit card required to start your trial.
How often is Monomidi updated?
Major updates are released quarterly, with bug fixes and minor improvements released as needed. All updates are free for licensed users.
What are the system requirements?
Minimum: 4GB RAM, 2GHz processor, 500MB disk space. Recommended: 8GB RAM, quad-core processor, SSD storage for optimal performance.
Can I integrate your software with my existing systems?
Most of my software includes API access and integration capabilities. Custom integration work is available as part of development or support packages.
Do you provide technical documentation?
Yes, comprehensive documentation is provided including user guides, API references, and integration examples. Documentation is kept up-to-date with each release.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and major credit cards. For larger projects, milestone-based payment schedules can be arranged.
Do you offer refunds?
Custom development projects include milestone approvals to ensure satisfaction. Product licenses include a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
Are there discounts for multiple projects?
Yes, I offer volume discounts for clients with multiple projects or ongoing development needs. Contact me to discuss custom pricing.
